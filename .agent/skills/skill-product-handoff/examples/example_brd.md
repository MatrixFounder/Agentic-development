# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

> **Project:** FlexArb Bots
> **Status:** Draft
> **Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
To define the requirements for a non-custodial crypto arbitrage bot.

### 1.2 Scope
- **In-Scope:** Spot market arbitrage, major 3 exchanges.
- **Out-of-Scope:** DeFi DEXs.

---

## 2. Business Overview
### 2.1 Problem Statement
Retail traders fear API key theft. Existing bots are custodial or closed source.

### 2.2 Product Vision
"For semi-pro traders, FlexArb is a safe vault..."

### 2.3 Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **North Star:** $10M Volume processed safely.

---

## 3. Market & Competition
### 3.1 Target Audience
Semi-pro traders with >$10k portfolio.

### 3.2 Competitive Landscape
- **Competitor A:** High fees, bad UX.
- **FlexArb:** Low fees, superior security.
